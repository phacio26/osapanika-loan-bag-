Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Iphushidwe" mean?

"Iphushidwe" is a Chichewa term meaning "Let it be pushed" or "Push it forward." Our loan is designed to do exactly that—push your business to the next level of success.

Who is eligible for this loan?

This loan is for registered small to medium-sized businesses operating in Malawi. You need to provide proof of business registration and show evidence of ongoing business activity.

What documents do I need to apply?

You will need your business registration certificate, proof of business activity (like bank statements or sales records), a valid National ID for the owner, and a clear plan for how you intend to use the loan funds.

How are the repayment terms structured?

Repayment terms are flexible, ranging from 3 to 12 months. We work with you to create a repayment schedule that aligns with your business's cash flow, whether it's monthly, quarterly, or seasonal.
