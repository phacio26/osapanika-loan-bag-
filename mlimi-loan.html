Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Mlimi Loan

What is a Mlimi Loan?

A Mlimi Loan is a specialized loan product designed specifically for farmers with favorable terms that align with agricultural cycles and needs. It provides financial support for various farming activities including purchasing inputs, equipment, and expanding operations.

What are the benefits?

The Mlimi Loan offers flexible repayment terms that can be adjusted to match your harvest cycles, competitive interest rates designed to support farmers, and financial assistance tailored specifically for agricultural activities to help you grow your farming business.

Who is eligible?

This loan is available to farmers with a viable farming business. You can be a small-scale or commercial farmer, growing crops or raising livestock. Both individual farmers and farming groups are eligible to apply.

What documents are required?

To apply, you'll need a valid ID (National ID, Passport, or Driver's License), proof of farming activity (this could be photos of your farm, previous sales receipts, or a letter from your agricultural extension worker), and a detailed budget outlining how you plan to use the loan funds.

How long does it take to process the loan?

Once you submit all required documents, we typically process Mlimi Loans within 24 hours. The funds will be disbursed directly to your account or through our mobile money partners for quick access.

Repayment terms that can be tailored to fit your farming cycle and financial situation. We understand that farming income comes in seasons, so we'll work with you to create a repayment schedule that matches your harvest periods.
